3PL & Fulfillment Audit

This service is for operators already shipping orders but dealing with slow delivery, refund pressure, broken coordination, or unclear 3PL decisions. The goal is to diagnose the actual bottleneck before changing the setup.

Best-fit clients

  • Shipping delays are causing refunds or complaints
  • The team is unsure whether to keep direct shipping, consolidate, or move to a 3PL
  • No one is fully coordinating suppliers, warehousing, and delivery
Deliverables

What this audit actually gives the client

Current-state diagnosis

  • Map the supplier, order handling, warehousing, packaging, and shipping path
  • Identify the nodes causing delays, refunds, and complaints
  • Assess whether the current model fits the current stage

Fulfillment recommendation

  • Decide whether direct supplier shipping still makes sense
  • Evaluate whether consolidation or repackaging is needed
  • Recommend whether a 3PL or a staged transition is the better move
